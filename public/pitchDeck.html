Core Paradigm

Instant Liquidity. Zero Middlemen.

Kryon brings commercial invoice factoring fully on-chain. We empower operating businesses to directly tokenize verified open invoices and draw down immediate, friction-free working capital from decentralized liquidity pools.

Workflow Execution

How It Works

01

Connect & Tokenize

The operating business cleanly syncs their verified accounting or ERP system to mint an immutable programmatic invoice token.

02

Cryptographic Verification

Zero-Knowledge (ZK) proofs actively validate invoice legitimacy and vendor records without exposing any sensitive or proprietary corporate data.

03

Instant Funding

Soroban smart contracts handle instantaneous settlement routing, deploying institutional USDC liquidity directly to the borrower's wallet.

Protocol Foundations

Powered by Stellar & Soroban

  • Soroban Smart Contracts: Custom-engineered, ultra-secure automated escrow logic and programmatic risk assessment layer written entirely in Rust.
  • Enterprise Execution Speed: Fast sub-second transaction finality paired with optimized, near-zero network transaction overhead fees.
  • Global Standard Assets: Fully native USDC integration providing a highly stable pool architecture alongside global asset on/off-ramps.
Liquidity Dynamics

Real-World Yield for DeFi Providers

  • DeFi Liquidity Providers (LPs) seamlessly supply liquidity by routing stable USDC directly into underlying systemic protocol pools.
  • Upon verified clearing and final corporate settlement of the invoice, the Soroban smart contract completely closes out the dynamic pool position.
  • Principal capital alongside the calculated organic discount interest rate flows straight back to pool LPs, delivering predictable yield backed by genuine economic activity.
